## TL;DR

There are plenty of great TODO apps out there. Each one has impressive features on its own, but when you try to do everything you personally need, you end up having to set up integrations, pull APIs, and write your own scripts -- it gets tedious fast.

This is inevitable. The world's needs and my own needs will never align perfectly. So I decided to build a TODO app that matches my needs exactly. There's a good chance I'll abandon it halfway through, but I'm going to document the journey as I go.
